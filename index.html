Form table

PTS / GAME
Points per game in the form window — 3 for a win, 1 for a draw. Higher is better.
W-D-L
Wins, draws and losses across the games counted.
xG FOR / AG
Expected goals created / conceded per game — the quality of chances made and allowed.
xG DIFF
xG for minus xG against. Positive means a team creates more than it gives up.
xG / SHOT
Average chance quality — high means good openings, not just lots of long-range efforts.
GLS F / A
Actual goals scored and conceded per game.
CRN F
Corners won per game.
POSS
Average share of possession.

Predictions

Predicted
The most likely scoreline from the model. Scores are spread out, so it's a best guess, not a lock.
xG (match)
Each side's expected goals for this match — the engine behind the score.
Top scores
The three most likely scorelines with their probabilities.
This score
Probability the exact predicted scoreline happens.
Over 2.5 / BTTS
Chance of 3+ total goals / both teams scoring.
Corners / Cards
Expected totals. o-lines show the chance of going over — o9.5 means 10 or more.
Confidence
How much xG-backed form data the prediction rests on. Low = thin evidence.

Value & accuracy

Market %
The bookmakers' implied probability with their margin removed — the sharpest benchmark there is.
Value / Edge
Model probability minus market probability. A positive edge means the model rates it more likely than the price implies. Edge is not a guarantee.
Brier
Average squared error of the result probabilities — lower is better (0 perfect, ~0.20 is decent).
Log-loss
Penalises confident wrong calls — lower is better.
Host edge
USA, Mexico and Canada get a home-advantage boost when playing in their own country.

Form table

PTS / GAME
Points per game in the form window — 3 for a win, 1 for a draw. A simple form rating; higher is better.
W-D-L
Wins, draws and losses across the games counted.
xG FOR
Expected goals created per game — the quality of chances a team makes. Higher means better chances.
xG AG
Expected goals conceded per game — the chances they let the opposition have. Lower is better.
xG DIFF
xG for minus xG against. Positive means a team creates more than it gives up — the single best form signal.
xG / SHOT
xG divided by shots — average chance quality. High means good openings, not just lots of long-range efforts.
GLS F / GLS A
Actual goals scored and conceded per game.
CRN F
Corners won per game.
POSS
Average share of possession.

Predictions

Predicted
The single most likely scoreline from the model. Football scores are spread out, so this is a best guess, not a lock.
xG (under score)
Each side's expected goals for this specific match — the engine behind the predicted score.
This score
The probability the exact predicted scoreline actually happens.
Over 2.5
Chance of three or more total goals in the match.
BTTS
Both teams to score.
Corners / Cards
Expected match totals. The o-lines show the chance of going over — e.g. o9.5 is the chance of 10 or more.
Confidence
How much xG-backed form data the prediction rests on. Low means thin evidence — read it with more caution.
All figures are recency-weighted averages from each team's recent games. Estimates, not certainties.

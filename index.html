Players

Each player's chance of scoring, from goal and shot rates sharing the team's expected goals.

Chance of a yellow, from cards and fouls per 90 plus the referee.
Penalty taker
The squad's likeliest spot-kick taker, from penalties won and scored.
Unavailable

Rating

Value & accuracy

Market %
Bookmakers' implied probability with the margin removed.
Value / Edge
Model probability minus market. Positive = model rates it likelier than the price. Not a guarantee.
Brier / Log-loss
How accurate the result probabilities have been — lower is better.
